📝 Grammar Practice

A. Present Perfect Continuous
Rule: Present Perfect Continuous (have/has been + verb-ing) shows an action that began in the past and is still happening or has recently finished, with the focus on duration or result now.
Example: She has been planting trees for an hour.
1. Choose the correct form:
Anna ___ (collect) rubbish for 2 hours!
2. Make the sentence in Present Perfect Continuous:
"He / clean out / the pond / since morning."
3. Find the mistake and write correctly:
She have been planting flowers for two hours.
B. Phrasal Verbs: make up, make up with, make out
Rule: Phrasal verbs consist of a verb and a particle(s) and often mean something special:
· make up – invent (придумать);
· make up with – become friends again (помириться);
· make out – understand with difficulty (разобрать).
1. Choose the correct answer:
After the fight, they decided to ___ .
2. Choose the correct phrasal verb:
I can't ___ what the sign says from here.
3. Translate (with a phrasal verb):
"Он выдумал эту историю."
4. Fill the gap:
Tom and Nick had a quarrel, but later they ___ .
C. Modal Verbs: have/has to, don’t/doesn’t have to
Rule: have to/has to means "necessary, required" (нужно, должен), don't/doesn't have to — "not necessary" (не обязательно).
Example: You don’t have to finish now. She has to clean the pond.
1. Choose the correct answer:
You ___ recycle cans if you don’t want to, but it’s better for the planet!
2. Complete:
She ___ collect rubbish every Friday. (insert "has to" or "doesn't have to")
3. Translate: "Ему не нужно поливать цветы."
D. Question Tags
Rule: Question tags — короткие вопросы (isn’t it? don’t they?) в конце предложения. Если утверждение положительное, тэг – отрицательный, и наоборот.
Example: She is recycling, isn't she? | They don’t have to go, do they?
1. Complete:
He doesn’t have to clean out the pond, ___ ?
2. Choose the correct tag:
She collects rubbish, ___ ?
3. Fill in the correct tag:
You have to water the flowers, ___ ?
