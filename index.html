📝 Grammar Practice

A. Present Perfect Continuous (+, -, ?)

Rule: have/has + been + V-ing.
(+) She has been planting trees.
(-) They haven't been recycling.
(?) Have you been cleaning the pond?
1. (+) Affirmative: They ___ (collect) rubbish since 9 AM.
2. (-) Negative: Write the negative sentence: "He / not / plant flowers / recently."
3. (?) Interrogative: Make a question: "you / read a book about ecology / all day?"

B. Phrasal Verbs (make)

make up = invent (придумать)
make up with = become friends again (помириться)
make out = understand/see with difficulty (разобрать)
1. I can't ___ what is written on the board.
2. Translate: "Они поссорились, но потом помирились."
They argued, but later they ___ .

C. Modals (have to / don't have to)

have to = it's necessary (нужно)
don't have to = it's NOT necessary (не обязательно)
1. Tomorrow is Sunday, so I ___ wake up early.
2. Insert the correct form: She ___ (обязана) clean out a pond today.

D. Question Tags

Positive statement -> Negative tag (She works, doesn't she?)
Negative statement -> Positive tag (He isn't, is he?)
1. We have to collect the rubbish, ___?
2. She doesn't have to emit toxic fumes, ___?
