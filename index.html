Grammar Practice

A. Present Perfect Continuous

Формы (Forms)

Используется для действий, которые начались в прошлом и всё ещё длятся, либо только что завершились и есть видимый результат.

TypeFormulaExample
(+) Affirmativehave/has + been + V-ingHe has been planting trees.
(-) Negativehave/has + not + been + V-ingWe haven't been recycling.
(?) QuestionHave/Has + subject + been + V-ing?Have you been reading?
1. (+) It ___ (rain) since morning, so the ground is wet.
2. (-) Make it negative: "They / clean out / the pond / for an hour." (Use short form)
3. (?) Make a question: "she / collect rubbish / all day?"

B. Phrasal Verbs (Make)

make up = invent a story/excuse (выдумывать)

make up with = become friends again after a fight (помириться)

make out = understand or see with difficulty (с трудом разобрать/понять)

1. It was dark, and I couldn't ___ the sign on the door.
2. That's not true! You just ___ the whole story! (Past Simple)

C. Modals & Question Tags

have to = necessary (нужно). don't have to = not necessary (не обязательно).

Tags: Positive sentence -> Negative tag (You read, don't you?). Negative sentence -> Positive tag (She isn't, is she?)

1. You ___ buy plastic bags, you can use paper ones. (Не обязательно)
2. We have to teach the cycle of life, ___? (Write the tag)
